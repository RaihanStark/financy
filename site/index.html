Private & local-first personal finance

Give every dollar a job.

Financy is a fast, private zero-based budgeting app for desktop and mobile. Assign every unit of money to a category until Ready to Assign hits zero, then track your envelopes through the month — all backed by real double-entry accounting, so your budget and balances can never drift apart. Your data lives in a single encrypted file you own. No cloud. No account. No tracking.

Zero-based budgeting, backed by real accounting

Everything you need to plan every dollar — with numbers that never lie.

🎯

Zero-based budget

Give every category a job until Ready to Assign hits zero. Track Assigned · Activity · Available per envelope, with balances that roll forward and overspending in red.

💰

Honest Ready to Assign

One global pool drawn from your real on-budget balances. Assign to any month — even a future one — and it draws down today. You can't budget money you don't have.

💳

Credit cards & tracking

Card spending nets against the budget so it stays budget-neutral. Mark investments or a mortgage as off-budget — counted in net worth, not in assignable cash.

📒

Real double-entry

Every transaction is balanced postings that sum to zero. Your budget, balances and net worth are derived from one journal — they can't go inconsistent.

🛡️

Optional encryption

Protect a document with a passphrase and the file is encrypted at rest with XChaCha20-Poly1305 and an Argon2id key. Decrypted only in memory — never in plaintext on disk.

🗓️

Debts & installments

Track BNPL and installment plans on a fixed schedule. Each debt becomes its own budget envelope and off-budget liability, so paying it down stays Ready-to-Assign neutral.

📊

Analytics

KPIs, income-vs-expenses, net-worth trends and spending by category — all derived from your journal, so they never drift from your transactions.

📱

Desktop & mobile

A native desktop app and a touch-first Android build (beta) from the same codebase — budget on your laptop, capture spending on your phone.

💾

Local-first & yours

Each document is a single .financy SQLite file, auto-saved with ACID writes. No cloud, no account, no save button — just your data on your machine.

Get Financy

Grab the build for your platform from the latest release. Builds are currently unsigned, so Windows/macOS may ask you to confirm before the first run.

🐧 Linux

Install the .deb or .rpm, or extract the .tar.xz and run.

🪟 Windows

Run the .exe. SmartScreen → More info → Run anyway.

🍎 macOS

Unzip, move to Applications. First launch: right-click → Open.

📱 Android

Beta .apk attached to releases. Allow install from unknown sources.
