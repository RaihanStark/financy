**Financy** is a fast, local-first **zero-based budgeting (ZBB)** app: give every
unit of money a job until *Ready to Assign* hits zero, then track your envelopes
through the month. Underneath it's **real double-entry accounting**, so your budget,
balances and net worth are all derived from one journal and can never drift out of
balance. Your data lives in a single file you own.

This is the user guide. New here? Start with
**[Getting Started]({{< relref "getting-started" >}})**, learn the
**[Budget]({{< relref "budget" >}})** workflow, then skim
**[Core Concepts]({{< relref "concepts" >}})** to understand the double-entry model
that makes the numbers trustworthy.
