The **Accounts** screen is your home base: a net-worth overview plus cards for
every money account.

![Accounts screen](../../img/accounts.png)

## Net worth overview

The banner shows **Net Worth**, **Total Assets**, and **Total Liabilities** — all
derived live from your transactions.

## Account cards

Assets and liabilities each get a card showing the account name, institution,
current balance, and transaction count. Liabilities are shown as positive amounts
owed.

- **Click a card** to open its **register** — the running-balance ledger for that
  account, newest first.
- **Right-click a card** (or use the **⋮** button) for *New Transaction · View
  Register · Edit Account · Delete Account*.

## Adding an account

Use **Add Account** (top-right). Choose **Asset** or **Liability**, optionally set
an institution and an **opening balance** (which posts an Opening Balance entry —
see [Concepts]({{< relref "concepts" >}})). The opening-balance field formats as you type.

## Deleting an account

An account can only be deleted once it has **no transactions** — remove or
reassign them first. This protects the integrity of your ledger.
