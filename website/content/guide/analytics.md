The **Analytics** screen is a read-only insights dashboard. Everything is derived
from your journal — no separate data to maintain.

![Analytics dashboard](../../img/analytics.png)

## Period selector

Pick the reporting window at the top: **This month**, **Last 3 / 6 / 12 months**,
or **Year to date**. Every figure and chart updates to match.

## KPI cards

- **Net Worth** — current, with the change over the period.
- **Income** and **Expenses** — totals for the period (plus average monthly spend).
- **Net Saved** — income minus expenses.
- **Savings Rate** — the share of income you kept.

## Charts

- **Income vs Expenses** — grouped monthly bars.
- **Net Worth Over Time** — a running trend line.
- **Spending by Category** — ranked bars with each category's share.

Charts are drawn natively (no web dependencies), with value gridlines and **hover
tooltips** showing the exact figures for any month.

{{< callout type="info" >}}
Because analytics is derived, it's always consistent with the journal — fix a
transaction and the dashboard updates instantly.
{{< /callout >}}
